Bio

After a miserable attempt at becoming Richard Feynman, I studied journalism and got a BA in media production management. Meanwhile, I did some work in page layout and design. After university I learnt how to make websites and started a company, mainly putting together Wordpress themes with Sage. Earlier this year my wife and I moved from Dublin, Ireland to San Antonio, Texas. When we're not complaining about the roads, we're marvelling about almost everything else.
